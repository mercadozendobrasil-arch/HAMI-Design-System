# Callout Component

## Purpose
Connect a real product detail to a concise label.

## Inputs
`anchor`, `label`, optional `iconId`, optional `detailCrop`.

## Slots
Anchor marker, connector and label or Feature Card.

## Tokens
`border.focus`, `font.feature`, `space.2`, `color.accent.primary`.

## QA
Reject anchors outside the real feature, crossing connectors or labels over two lines.

## Dependencies / Related / Version
`Arrow.md`, `FeatureCard.md`; used by Templates002, 004, 005 and 006. Version `2.0.0`.
